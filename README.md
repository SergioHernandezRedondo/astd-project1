## Goal of the project

You work at a small company dedicated to providing innovative data analysis and visualization tools to public and private companies of different domains. ALLSTAT the public company for official statistics is trying out different companies to develop advanced data visualization tools for their data. As a trial, the company has provide a sample of its data, contained in file CO2.xlsx. The objective is to develop a prototype visualization Dashboard for this dataset.

## Minimum requirements

1. Exploration/visualization of the data.
  1. All the data must be visualized/explored somehow.
  2. At least three different types of graphics must be used.
  3. At least one non-basic graphic must be used (histograms, scatter-plots, pie-charts, box-plots etc. are basic).

2. Compilation of results.
  1. All results (tables, graphics, etc.) must be shown in a dashboard.
